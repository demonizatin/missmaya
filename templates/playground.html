All 11 buckets — what they are and how they grow

Bucket What it holds Lifecycle
factsTimeless truths: name, profession, family, hometown, interests, past experiences.Forever, until contradicted. Name is overwrite-protected.
eventsDated things: exams, weddings, matches, appointments.Auto-archive 14 days after the date.
momentsEmotionally weighted things you said (no date) — fears, prides, ongoing strains. Sticky sensitive flag for acute disclosures.Persist long-term. Dedupe + bump mentions.
mood_logOne mood reading per session — label + energy + confidence.Last 14 entries. Newest in, oldest out.
cooldownWhat Maya raised recently — topics, opener kinds, opener phrases — so she doesn't repeat herself.Fixed-size FIFOs (12 / 10 / 6).
loreInside jokes / shared callbacks between Maya and you.Up to 30 entries. Dormant items can resurface.
anticipation_queuePre-cooked openers Maya plans to use next time — pulled from upcoming events, recent moments, etc.Up to 5. TTL 14 days. Consumed on use.
skillsYour English mistakes (as patterns) and wins, plus a derived "current focus" list.Up to 20 errors / 30 wins. Patterns flip to fixed when you stop slipping.
maya_personaMaya's character. shared_with_user tracks self-details she's already revealed to you, so she doesn't repeat them.Decoration only — never claims biography.
open_loopsThreads either of you said you'd come back to ("tell me how the meeting went").Up to 12. Auto-expire 30 days unfollowed.
meta_preferencesKnobs you explicitly set: correction style, reply length, humor level, off-limits topics.Hard override. User's word is law. Maya can't ask you for these.

Each section in the rendered block on the right maps 1:1 to a bucket above. Empty buckets are omitted from the prompt entirely (no "About you" header for a brand-new user).

📅 Three growth patterns — which buckets behave how

Just keeps growing (with dedup)

  • facts.* list fields (interests, etc.) — append-only with dedup
  • moments — dedupes by exact text, bumps mentions on repeat

New stuff added; nothing leaves until you explicitly contradict.

Newest in, oldest out (FIFO)

  • mood_log — last 14 sessions
  • cooldown.recent_topics — last 12
  • cooldown.recent_openers — last 10
  • cooldown.recent_opening_phrases — last 6
  • lore — up to 30
  • skills.error_patterns — up to 20
  • skills.wins — up to 30
  • maya_persona.shared_with_user

Fixed-size buffers; oldest entry drops as new arrives.

Items leave on their own

  • events — auto-archive 14 days after date
  • anticipation_queue — TTL 14 days; consumed on use
  • open_loops — TTL 30 days unfollowed

Items expire by date or get consumed — not pushed out by new ones.

🔑 Update Bedrock token

AWS Bedrock bearer tokens are short-lived (~12 hours). Paste a fresh one and save — no restart needed. Both prod and playground pick it up immediately.

Tip: if this gets annoying, set long-lived IAM credentials in Settings instead — those don't expire on a clock.
